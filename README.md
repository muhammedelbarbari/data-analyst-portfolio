## 📊 Featured Project  
### Sales Performance Analysis & Optimization

**Description:**  
End‑to‑end sales analysis to identify profit leakages and propose data‑driven strategies to improve overall profitability.

**Dataset:** Superstore Sales Dataset  
**Period:** 2014 – 2017  
**Total Records:** 9,994 transactions

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Profitability was negatively impacted due to **high discounts** and **poor performance** in specific products and regions. The goal was to find clear opportunities to improve profit without increasing sales volume.

---

## ✅ Objectives
- Analyze sales and profit performance.
- Identify root causes of losses.
- Propose practical, data‑driven strategies to increase profit.

---

## 📈 Key Results
- **Original Profit:** 286,397  
- **Identified Improvement Opportunity:** 251,453  
- **Potential Profit Increase:** 87.8%  
- **Projected New Profit:** 537,850  

---

## 🚀 Strategic Initiatives
1. Stop selling Furniture in loss‑making regions.
2. Reduce extreme discounts on unprofitable products.
3. Replace heavy discounts with a low‑cost shipping strategy.
4. Focus growth efforts on the top 5 most profitable products.

---

## 💡 Business Impact
- Improved pricing and discount decisions.
- Increased profitability without increasing sales.
- Clear, actionable recommendations for management.
